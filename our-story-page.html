Our Story

Honey Bee Sweets is locally owned and has been part of Atlanta for over 2 years. We love Atlanta and are committed to the local community.

We strive to use the highest quality ingredients possible. Ingredients that not only are sourced for their level of flavor, but are also responsible for our community and our world. Farmers Market fresh fruits and vegetables, organic sugars, and local flour when available. Finely crafted Belgian chocolates, and pure Vanilla from Madagascar.

Honey Bee Sweets is at a convenient, laid back central location that is perfect for lingering with the paper or grabbing some quick coffee and breakfast on your way to work.
